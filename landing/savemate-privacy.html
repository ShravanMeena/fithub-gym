SaveMate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026  ·  Effective: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SaveMate ("SaveMate", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the SaveMate mobile application (the "App"). SaveMate is a tool that lets you download videos, reels, and audio from public links you paste into the App and save them to your device.

SaveMate does not require you to create an account or sign in. We designed the App to collect as little information as possible.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Links (URLs) you paste — to fetch the media you want, the link you paste is processed by the App and sent to our servers and/or trusted third-party services that resolve the direct media file. We use the link only to perform the download you requested.

Information collected automatically

CategoryDetails
Technical & log dataBasic information such as app version, device type, operating-system version, language, and error/crash logs, used to operate, secure, and improve the App.
Usage analyticsAggregated, non-identifying events (e.g. a download started or failed) to understand feature usage and fix problems.

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, precise location, or social-media login credentials. SaveMate never asks you to log in to Instagram, Facebook, X, or any other platform.

2. Files you download

Videos, reels, and audio you download are saved directly on your device (your gallery and the App's library). We do not upload, copy, host, or keep your downloaded files on our servers, and we cannot see the media in your library. Managing, sharing, or deleting those files happens entirely on your device.

3. Permissions we request

  • Storage / Media (Photos & Videos) — to save your downloads to your device and show them in your library. On newer Android versions this may use the system's scoped media access.
  • Internet / Network access — required to fetch the media from the link you paste.
  • Notifications (optional) — to show download progress and completion.

You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings; some features may stop working without them.

4. How we use information

  • Resolve the link you paste and perform the requested download.
  • Show download progress and manage your on-device library.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the App (using technical and analytics data).

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data only with:

  • Service providers that help us run the App — for example, hosting/CDN providers used to resolve and deliver the media you requested, and analytics/crash-reporting providers. They process data only to provide their services to us.
  • Advertising partners — if the App displays ads, ad providers may collect limited device identifiers to serve and measure ads, in line with their own privacy policies. (See note below — update this if the App is ad-free.)
  • Legal — where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.

6. Data retention

Because SaveMate has no accounts, we do not maintain a personal profile about you. Pasted links are used only to complete your download and are not stored long-term in a form that identifies you. Technical/analytics logs are kept only as long as needed to operate and improve the App and are then deleted or aggregated.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard measures such as encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and reputable infrastructure to protect data in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. Children's privacy

SaveMate is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will address it.

9. Your choices

  • Manage or delete downloaded files anytime from your device library or gallery.
  • Turn off notifications or revoke permissions in your device settings.
  • Reset your advertising ID in your device settings to limit ad personalization.

10. Copyright & acceptable use

SaveMate is a general-purpose tool for saving media. You are responsible for how you use it. Please only download content that you created or that you have the right or permission to download, and respect the rights of content owners and the terms of service of each platform. SaveMate is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Instagram, Facebook, X, or any other platform, and those names are trademarks of their respective owners.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of SaveMate after changes means you accept the updated policy.
